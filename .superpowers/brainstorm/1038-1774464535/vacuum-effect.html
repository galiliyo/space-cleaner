Vacuum Effect — Which look?

The current effect: 8 tiny cyan dots drifting inward. What should it become?

A — Spiral Pull

More particles (20+), visible spiral motion inward, pulsing center glow. Dashed guide rings hint at the vacuum radius. Builds on current approach, just more of it.

Stretched trail particles that look like speed lines being sucked in. Spinning ring at center. More dramatic and "powerful" feeling. Uses stretched billboard rendering.

Gentle glittering field around the ship. Soft ambient glow underneath. Feels magical/dreamy. Less aggressive than A/B — better if the vacuum is "always on" background ambiance.
